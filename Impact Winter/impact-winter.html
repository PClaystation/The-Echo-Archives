Summary

Impact Winter is a post-apocalyptic audio drama set 7 years after a comet hit Earth. The survivors struggle to adapt to a frozen world filled with dangers both natural and supernatural. It’s an emotional and thrilling experience that captures the fight for survival in the harshest conditions imaginable.

Own Thoughts

I absolutely loved this podcast. The combination of voice acting, sound design, and story made it one of my top recommendations. The way the narrative shifts perspectives kept it interesting throughout, and the atmosphere is haunting yet immersive. Highly recommend it!
