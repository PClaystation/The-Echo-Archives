Summary

Impact Winter Impact Winter is a post-apocalyptic audio drama set seven years after a comet slammed into Earth, plunging the world into a devastating winter and endless darkness. But that wasn't the only nightmare unleashed, from the shadows came bloodthirsty monsters. Vampires.
The year is 2042. Darci and her younger sister Hope live in a bunker beneath a medieval castle, surviving alongside a small group of others. They make do with what they have and hold onto the hope that the sun will return someday.
But in this brutal new world, nothing is as it seems. Are the vampires truly the monsters everyone fears? What's really happening behind the scenes? Will the sun ever come back? Welcome to Impact Winter where survival is only the beginning.

Own Thoughts

I absolutely loved Impact Winter. It really grabs you by the throat and pulls you in. Season 1 was absolutely amazing, but it tapered off a bit towards season 3 but overall was still an amazing listen. Would recommend to anyone looking for a new audio drama to listen to.
